About Me

I'm a UX designer with 3 years of experience creating digital products that people love to use. I have a Master's Degree from ASU in User Experience. My background in Human Systems Engineering, Graphic Design, and Technical Writing gives me a unique perspective on how people interact with technology.

I specialize in user research, interaction design, and creating design systems. My process always starts with understanding the user to meet their needs.

“When I’m not designing, I’m chasing adrenaline on a mountain bike or snowboard, and channeling my creative side through decorating, fashion, and cake design.
